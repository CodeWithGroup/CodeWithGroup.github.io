Newsletter Process

Anyone can submit a section to be sent to the mailing list, either in a specific email or in the next newsletter

Use of the mailing list

Anything submitted to be sent out to the mailing list must meet one of the below criteria

  • Be relavant for everyone on the mailing list
  • If event details are in the section, it must have followed the CodeWith Event Process
