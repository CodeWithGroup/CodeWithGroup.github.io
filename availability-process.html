Availability Process

Mentors are expected to fill in their availability to help at CodeWith events. We recognise this may change but it gives the event coordinator a starting point to find mentors.

A monthly reminder will be sent out in the #admin and #mentor channel on Slack to update the spreadsheet and add new months as the year moves on.
