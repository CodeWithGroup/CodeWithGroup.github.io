Welcome to CodeWith!

Our mission is to create a virtual space for adults to pursue programming-related learning and conversation, where everyone is valued, everyone has agency, and everyone is supported.

Want to dive right in? We usually have a few people connected to our Slack workspace, so come and speak to us there!

We started the group in the midst of COVID-19 and so all our activities are currently online. We hope to eventually host some events in person, but until then it'll all be done via keyboard, microphone, and/or webcam.

For more information about the workshops, events, and drop-ins, have a look at the events page.

We offer a friendly space where anyone, from complete beginners to accomplished software engineers, can get together with like-minded people to learn and share a passion for coding. For more information, check out our about page.

Stay up to date! There will be a lot of announcements over the coming weeks and the best way to hear about what we're doing is to sign up to our mailing list. There's a form where you can give us your details on our contact page.

We'd love to hear your feedback. Let us know your thoughts on our feedback form.
