Welcome to CodeWith!

Our mission is to create a virtual space for adults to pursue programming-related learning and conversation, where everyone is valued, everyone has agency, and everyone is supported.

Want to dive right in? We usually have a few people connected to our Slack workspace, so come and speak to us there. If you're not signed up, you can join our Slack here.

Make sure you get notified of our events! Follow us on Eventbrite and you'll get an email whenever we have something new to offer. Thanks to COVID-19 all our activities are currently online. We hope to eventually host some events in person, but until then it'll all be done via keyboard, microphone, and/or webcam. For more information on the types of event we run, have a look at the events page.

We offer a friendly space where anyone, from complete beginners to accomplished software engineers, can get together with like-minded people to learn and share a passion for coding. For more information, check out our about page.

We send out annoucements and updates from time to time. The best way to hear about what we're doing is to sign up to our mailing list. There's a form where you can give us your details on our contact page.

We'd love to hear your feedback. Let us know your thoughts on our feedback form.
